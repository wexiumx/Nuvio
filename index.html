NUVIO

Nuvio is a technology company specializing in designing and selling smartphones that combine cutting-edge features with sleek, modern designs, focusing on innovation, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

About us

Nuvio was founded in 2026 by two inspired students with a vision to create smartphones offering the best price-to-performance ratio on the market. Our goal is to deliver devices that combine powerful hardware with an optimized operating system, built on the solid foundation of Arch Linux. Our OS is designed to be fast, secure, and user-friendly, providing a seamless experience for both casual users and tech enthusiasts. We draw design inspiration from popular tech companies, creating sleek, elegant devices that feel premium without the premium price. By focusing on performance, innovation, and thoughtful design, we aim to set a new standard for modern smartphones.

Why choose us?

Performance

Our devices are designed for both everyday tasks and demanding applications, offering a premium experience without the premium cost.

Design & Usability
